Create your Habits Scorecard

The Habits Scorecard is a simple exercise we can use to become more aware of our behavior.

It is based on the “point-and-call” system and helps us become aware of our current habits by listing them out.

It encourages self-reflection and provides a visual representation of habits, aiding in the process of habit
formation and improvement.
